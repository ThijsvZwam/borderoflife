GAMEPLAY

ACTION COMBAT
Fast, fluid hack-and-slash combat that rewards aggression and precision. Chain attacks, read enemy patterns, and tear through the Gates before they tear through you.
💥
COMBO CHAINS & ULTIMATES
String together devastating combos that build toward explosive ultimate abilities. The longer you stay in rhythm, the harder you hit — break the chain and pay the price.

DESTROY THE GATES
Hunt down and shatter the Gates corrupting each dungeon. Every Gate is a gauntlet with its own rules — crack them open and face whatever's been hiding inside.

DUNGEON CRAWLING
Descend into shifting, treacherous dungeons that warp reality the deeper you go. Each floor holds secrets, ambushes, and rewards for those bold enough to push further.

// THE NARRATIVE
STORY

Somewhere between the waking world and oblivion, there exists a Border.

For most, it is invisible — a boundary they'll only cross once, at the very end. But ONYX can see it. Has always seen it. And lately, the Gates along that Border have been breaking open.

Something is leaking through. Something is hiding behind the faces of ordinary people, warping their minds from within. And the only way to stop it is to go beyond — to step across the Border of Life and unmask whatever waits in the dark.

What are they hiding? You'll have to cross the border to find out.
